Use of the App

RailPrep provides study material, mock tests, previous-year paper links, explanations, bookmarks, and progress tools for RRB NTPC exam preparation. The app is an independent educational product and is not affiliated with the Railway Recruitment Boards or the Government of India.

Educational Disclaimer

RailPrep is an independent exam-preparation app. It does not represent, is not affiliated with, and is not endorsed by any Railway Recruitment Board, Indian Railways, Ministry of Railways or the Government of India.

Official source links for government exam information: RRB Chandigarh official notices and CENs: https://www.rrbcdg.gov.in/; official list of all Railway Recruitment Boards: https://www.rrbcdg.gov.in/other-rrb.php; official NTPC CEN 06/2024 syllabus/pattern PDF: https://www.rrbcdg.gov.in/uploads/2024/06-NTPCUG/Detailed%20CEN%2006-2024%20NTPC.pdf; NCERT textbook source: https://ncert.nic.in/textbook.php.

RailPrep aims to be accurate and useful, but it does not guarantee selection, exam qualification, rank, job placement, or any specific score. Users should verify official notices, eligibility, exam dates, admit cards, answer keys, results and final notices from the relevant official RRB website.

Content and External Links

Some PYQ PDFs are opened from public external URLs. RailPrep links to those files and does not re-host them. If a linked file becomes unavailable, RailPrep may mark it stale or replace the link. NCERT and public-reference material is used or linked according to its source posture.

Accounts

Users are responsible for maintaining account access and using accurate sign-in details. Misuse, attempts to bypass access controls, scraping, bulk extraction, or redistribution of app content may lead to account restrictions.

Subscriptions and Refunds

If RailPrep Pro subscriptions are enabled, purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. Product IDs are rrb_ntpc_pro_monthly and rrb_ntpc_pro_quarterly. Refunds follow Google Play policies, including the standard Play refund path. Users can manage or cancel subscriptions from Google Play.

Account Deletion and Data Export

Users can export data or delete their account from Profile > Privacy & account. Deletion is permanent and removes user-owned app data as described in the Privacy Policy.
