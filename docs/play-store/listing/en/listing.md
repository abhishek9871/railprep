Official source links for government exam information:
- RRB Chandigarh official notices and CENs: https://www.rrbcdg.gov.in/
- Official list of all Railway Recruitment Boards: https://www.rrbcdg.gov.in/other-rrb.php
- Official NTPC CEN 06/2024 syllabus/pattern PDF: https://www.rrbcdg.gov.in/uploads/2024/06-NTPCUG/Detailed%20CEN%2006-2024%20NTPC.pdf
- NCERT textbook source: https://ncert.nic.in/textbook.php

Always verify exam dates, eligibility, admit cards, answer keys, results and final notices from the official RRB website above or your regional RRB.

RailPrep is built for serious RRB NTPC aspirants preparing for CBT-1 and CBT-2.

Practice with full-length mocks, topic sectionals, previous-year paper PDFs, saved questions, detailed explanations, bilingual learning material and weak-topic review.

What you get:
- CBT-1 and CBT-2 mock tests with exam-style timing and negative marking.
- Topic sectionals across Mathematics, Reasoning and General Awareness.
- In-app PYQ PDF library for RRB NTPC papers.
- Daily 10-question revision digest.
- Full review with correct answers, trap reasons and explanations.
- Saved Questions with editable notes.
- NCERT and primer-based learning material.
- English and Hindi app language support.
