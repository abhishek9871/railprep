# RailPrep Play Listing - Hindi

## App Name
RailPrep

## Short Description
RRB NTPC mocks, PYQ PDFs, sectionals और smart review.

## Full Description
RailPrep serious RRB NTPC aspirants के लिए बनाया गया exam-preparation app है।

CBT-1 और CBT-2 mocks, topic sectionals, previous-year paper PDFs, saved questions, detailed explanations, bilingual learning material और weak-topic review एक ही app में मिलते हैं।

आपको मिलता है:
- CBT-1 और CBT-2 mock tests with exam-style timer और negative marking.
- Mathematics, Reasoning और General Awareness के topic sectionals.
- RRB NTPC PYQ PDF library, app के अंदर ही.
- Daily 10-question revision digest.
- Review screen with correct answer, trap reasons और explanations.
- Saved Questions with editable notes.
- NCERT और primer-based learning material.
- English और Hindi app language support.

RailPrep independent exam-preparation app है। यह Railway Recruitment Boards या Government of India से affiliated नहीं है। Official notices, exam dates, eligibility और final answer keys हमेशा official RRB websites से verify करें।
