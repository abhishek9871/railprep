## Full Description
IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER: RailPrep independent exam-preparation app है। यह किसी Railway Recruitment Board, Indian Railways, Ministry of Railways या Government of India को represent नहीं करता, affiliated नहीं है और endorsed नहीं है।

Government exam information के official source links:
- RRB Chandigarh official notices और CENs: https://www.rrbcdg.gov.in/
- सभी Railway Recruitment Boards की official list: https://www.rrbcdg.gov.in/other-rrb.php
- Official NTPC CEN 06/2024 syllabus/pattern PDF: https://www.rrbcdg.gov.in/uploads/2024/06-NTPCUG/Detailed%20CEN%2006-2024%20NTPC.pdf

Exam dates, eligibility, admit cards, answer keys, results और final notices हमेशा ऊपर दिए official RRB website या अपने regional RRB से verify करें।

RailPrep serious RRB NTPC aspirants के लिए बनाया गया exam-preparation app है।

CBT-1 और CBT-2 mocks, topic sectionals, previous-year paper PDFs, saved questions, detailed explanations, bilingual learning material और weak-topic review एक ही app में मिलते हैं।

आपको मिलता है:
- CBT-1 और CBT-2 mock tests with exam-style timer और negative marking.
- Mathematics, Reasoning और General Awareness के topic sectionals.
- RRB NTPC PYQ PDF library, app के अंदर ही.
- Daily 10-question revision digest.
- Review screen with correct answer, trap reasons और explanations.
- Saved Questions with editable notes.
- NCERT और primer-based learning material.
- English और Hindi app language support.
