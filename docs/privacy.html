RailPrep Privacy Policy

Effective date: 26 April 2026

RailPrep is an Android exam-preparation app for RRB NTPC aspirants. This policy explains what data the app collects, why it is collected, and how users can control it.

Data We Collect

DataPurposeRetention
Email address and Google profile basics when used for sign-inAccount creation, authentication, and restoring progress across devicesUntil the user deletes the account
Exam goal, language choice, streaks, notification preferencePersonalising the study experienceUntil changed by the user or account deletion
Test attempts, answers, scores, review history, saved questions, bookmarks and notesShowing results, weak-topic routing, saved-question review, and progress trackingUntil account deletion
Optional local notification settingsDaily study reminder on the deviceUntil disabled by the user
Error logs without email, phone number, or free-text notesDiagnosing crashes and reliability problems7 days

What We Do Not Collect

RailPrep does not collect phone contacts, SMS, precise location, photos, microphone recordings, advertising identifiers, or payment-card details. Subscription payments are handled by Google Play Billing.

Third Parties

RailPrep uses Supabase for authentication, database storage, edge functions and operational logs. Google Play handles billing and app distribution. Google sign-in is used only when the user chooses it. RailPrep does not sell personal data and does not include third-party advertising SDKs.

Security

Data is transferred over HTTPS/TLS. Supabase stores production data with platform-level access controls. Row Level Security restricts user-owned records so users can access only their own private data.

User Rights

Users can export their data or delete their account from Profile > Privacy & account. Account deletion removes profile data, attempts, answers, bookmarks, saved questions, entitlements and user-linked error logs. Users may also contact support at railprep.support@gmail.com.

Children

RailPrep is intended for exam aspirants and is not directed at children under 13. Users under 18 should use the app with parent or guardian consent where applicable.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app changes. Material changes will be reflected on this page.
